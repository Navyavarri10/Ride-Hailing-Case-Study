# 🚖 Ola/Uber User Retention Case Study

> A Product Analytics case study focused on improving **user retention** and **engagement** in ride-hailing platforms like Ola and Uber through data-driven product thinking and prioritization frameworks.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Ride-hailing platforms experience strong first-time adoption but often struggle with **long-term user retention**. Many riders stop using the platform after only a few rides and frequently switch between competitors based on price or wait time.

The objective of this case study is to identify **low-cost, high-impact product solutions** that improve:

- 90-day user retention
- Repeat ride frequency
- Customer loyalty

without relying primarily on discounts.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Design and prioritize product features that:

- Increase repeat ride bookings
- Encourage long-term platform loyalty
- Reduce user churn
- Improve customer experience
- Minimize implementation cost

---

## 👤 Target User

**Urban Commuter**

- Age: 25–40 years
- Uses ride-hailing services 3–6 times per month
- Common use cases:
  - Daily office commute
  - Airport travel
  - Weekend outings

---

# 💡 Product Insights

Users don't stop needing rides.

They simply **don't have a strong reason to remain loyal** to one platform when competitors offer similar pricing and ETAs.

Therefore, the problem is not demand.

The problem is **retention and habit formation.**

---

# 🚀 Proposed Solutions

## 1️⃣ RideStreak

A habit-building loyalty system that rewards users for taking at least one ride every week.

### Rewards

- Priority pickup
- Surprise ride upgrades
- Better driver matching
- Exclusive badges

### Expected Impact

- Builds user habits
- Encourages repeat rides
- Improves long-term retention

---

## 2️⃣ Smart Recall ⭐ (Recommended Feature)

Predicts the user's next ride based on historical behavior.

Example:

> "Book your usual 6 PM ride home?"

Users can book in a single tap.

### Benefits

- Reduces booking friction
- Saves user time
- Prevents users from opening competitor apps
- Increases booking convenience

---

## 3️⃣ Driver Familiarity Match

Matches riders with previously highly-rated drivers whenever possible.

### Benefits

- Builds trust
- Improves ride comfort
- Creates emotional loyalty
- Improves perceived safety

---

# 📊 Feature Prioritization

## Effort vs Impact Matrix

| Feature | Impact | Effort | Priority |
|----------|---------|---------|-----------|
| Smart Recall | High | Low | ✅ Quick Win |
| RideStreak | High | Medium | Major Project |
| Driver Familiarity Match | Medium | High | Long-Term Investment |

### Recommended Roadmap

1. Smart Recall
2. RideStreak
3. Driver Familiarity Match

---

# 📈 RICE Prioritization

RICE Score

```
(Reach × Impact × Confidence) ÷ Effort
```

| Feature | Reach | Impact | Confidence | Effort | RICE Score |
|----------|-------|---------|------------|---------|------------|
| Smart Recall | 8 | 8 | 90% | 2 | **28.8** |
| RideStreak | 7 | 9 | 75% | 4 | 11.8 |
| Driver Familiarity Match | 4 | 6 | 60% | 6 | 2.4 |

### Winner

✅ **Smart Recall**

Reasons:

- Highest reach
- High impact
- Highest confidence
- Lowest implementation effort
